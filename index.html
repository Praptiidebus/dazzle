About Us

At GhumGham, we believe travel is more than just reaching a destination – it's about discovering new cultures, creating lifelong memories, and experiencing the world through a different lens. "GhumGham" means "traveling" in Nepali, and our name reflects our passion for exploration and our deep connection to Nepal's rich culture and natural beauty.

Who We Are

Founded by passionate travelers, GhumGham is a Nepal-based travel and tour company dedicated to offering personalized and immersive travel experiences. Whether you're a local adventurer or an international explorer, our goal is to help you discover the hidden gems of Nepal and beyond.

What We Offer

At GhumGham, we offer a wide range of travel services including customized tours, trekking adventures, cultural and heritage trips, city sightseeing, and adventure activities like paragliding, rafting, and jungle safaris. Whether you're exploring Nepal or traveling abroad, we craft experiences that suit your interests, pace, and budget.

Our Vision

Our vision at GhumGham is to inspire meaningful travel by offering authentic, safe, and responsible experiences. We aim to be a trusted name in travel by connecting people to the beauty of Nepal and the world, while supporting local communities and promoting sustainable tourism.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

We believe in delivering genuine travel experiences that reflect the true culture, traditions, and spirit of each destination.

Customer-Centricity

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen carefully to your needs and tailor every trip to match your preferences and expectations.

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible tourism that protects the environment, respects local communities, and preserves natural and cultural heritage for future generations.

Safety & Comfort

Your well-being matters most. We ensure all trips prioritize safety, comfort, and peace of mind from start to finish.
